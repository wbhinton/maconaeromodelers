### First Steps for New Members

If you are new to the club, we recommend starting with these three steps:

1.  **Read the Bylaws:** Understand how the club is run.
2.  **Check Safety Requirements:** Ensure you have your AMA card and FAA registration current.
3.  **Visit the Field:** Come out to 516 Tessentee Road and introduce yourself!
