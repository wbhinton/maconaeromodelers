Safety is the primary objective of Macon Aero Modelers. All members are expected to operate their aircraft in a manner that ensures the safety of themselves, other pilots, and spectators.

The following rules are extracted directly from the Club Constitution and Bylaws.

## Mandatory Requirements

### AMA Membership
Proof of insurance is non-negotiable for flying at our field.

* **Requirement:** All members must be a current member with the Academy of Model Aeronautics (AMA).
* **Verification:** You must be able to produce your AMA membership card upon request.
* **Scope:** AMA membership is required for **all** fliers.

<Aside type="danger" title="No Insurance, No Fly">
    Failure to maintain active AMA membership invalidates your permission to fly at the Tessentee Road field.
</Aside>

### FAA Compliance
In addition to AMA rules, the club adheres to federal law.
* It is recommended that members comply with current Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) regulations and requirements for model aviation.

---

## Visitor Policy

We welcome guest pilots to experience our flying site under specific conditions.

* **Frequency:** A person may fly as a visitor up to **three times** in a calendar year without paying dues or fees.
* **Insurance:** Visitors **must** provide proof of AMA membership to verify that they have insurance before flying.

---

## Enforcement & Authority

The **Safety Officer** is the designated authority for interpreting and enforcing rules at the field.

### Role of the Safety Officer
* **Authority:** The Safety Officer works to enforce the rules and regulations of this Club and the AMA at all activities.
* **Fines:** The Safety Officer has the authority to assess appropriate fines for repeated violations.
* **Resolution:** Complaints or issues relating to safe flying operations should be brought directly to the Safety Officer for resolution.

<Aside type="caution" title="Disciplinary Action">
    Repeated violations of rules may be reported to the Board of Directors. Failure to observe club objectives and rules may result in disciplinary action or expulsion from membership.
</Aside>

### AMA Safety Code
The Safety Officer is responsible for keeping up to date with all AMA safety rules. As a chartered AMA Gold Leader Club, we follow the **Official AMA National Model Aircraft Safety Code**.

* [View Official AMA Safety Code](https://www.modelaircraft.org/sites/default/files/105.pdf)
