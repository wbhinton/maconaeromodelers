## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does this take?
Generally, if the application is sent directly to the Treasurer, the process takes approximately **one week**.
* *Note: This time may be significantly extended if you do not deliver the package directly to the Treasurer.*

### Can I fly while my application is "In-Process"?
While not an official written policy, it is generally considered reasonable to allow an applicant to fly **only if**:
1.  The application and dues have been handed in.
2.  **AMA Status** has been verified.
3.  **Ability to fly safely** has been verified by a current member.
